# Electronics-Store-Business-Analysis
Business-focused data analytics project using Excel Pivot Tables and diagnosis-oriented reasoning to analyze retained revenue, return rates, customer behavior, and delivery performance across product categories. Includes KPI design, operational recommendations, and revenue retention analysis.
📁 Project Overview

<br>
This project focuses on analyzing an electronics store dataset using Excel Pivot Tables and business-oriented analytical reasoning. The objective was to identify which product categories were actually retaining revenue after discounts and returns, diagnose operational issues affecting business performance, and recommend measurable business actions and KPIs.
<br>
<br>


Business Problem:

<br>
The store owner observed that overall sales looked healthy, but business performance still felt inconsistent.
The analysis aimed to answer:
<br>


1.Which product categories are actually retaining revenue?
<br>
2.Which categories are heavily affected by returns and discounts?
<br>
3.What operational issues are likely causing revenue leakage?
<br>
4.Which KPIs should the business monitor weekly?
<br>

📊 Key Findings:

<br>
Accessories:
<br>
1.Retained only 23.53% revenue after discounts and returns
<br>
2.Highest return rate: 66.67%
<br>
3.Repeated Boat Earbuds returns suggested likely product-quality issues
<br>
4.Recommended supplier quality review and internal product testing
<br>
<br>

Laptops:
<br>
1.Retained 68.85% revenue
<br>
2.Lenovo Ideapad return likely linked to expectation mismatch or quality concerns
<br>
3.Recommended improved product specification visibility and quality checks
<br>
<br>

Mobile Phones:
<br>
1.Strongest-performing category
<br>
2.95.19% retained revenue
<br>
3.0% return rate
<br>
4.Fastest delivery timelines
<br>
5.Recommended focusing on customer retention and maintaining operational quality
<br>
<br>
KPIs Designed:
<br>
1.Product-wise Return Rate
<br>
2.Repeat Purchase Rate of Mobile Customers
<br>
<br>
Business Recommendations:
<br>
1.Introduce supplier quality audits
<br>
2.Improve product specification clarity
<br>
3.Add pre-purchase customer Q&A support
<br>
4.Implement internal quality testing before dispatch
<br>
5.Focus on retention strategies for satisfied mobile customers
<br>
<br>
